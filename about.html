Arc Index
Arc Index is a website that showcase a collection of diagram animations of computational design thinkings in Architecture field. With these design patterns, one may make our built environment more responsive to the changing environment and embrace more possibile spatial language in the design process. I hope this website can open up a design space bigger than the add up of isolated projects or precedent studies to accumulate bigger ideas behind the studies from school and career.



                                                    By Qiushi Chen(Architect in Train @ Carnegie Mellon)
